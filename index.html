About me

I'm a Data Engineer from India, currently in the US. My passion lies in building data infrastructure that transforms how businesses make decisions. To be honest, I wasn't sold on data engineering during my Bachelor's. I was more fascinated by data analytics and understanding patterns in data. Everything changed during my Master's when I discovered the power of turning raw data into business gold through robust engineering systems. For anyone looking to get into tech, data engineering is where the magic happens. There's never been a better time to build systems that actually matter.

I have one of the most exciting introductions to enterprise data engineering. Landing a role at GE Healthcare put me at the forefront of healthcare analytics, working alongside some of the brightest minds in the industry. The fascinating part? Building real-time data pipelines from scratch that directly impact patient outcomes. I've learned an incredible amount about streaming architectures, ML at scale, and cloud infrastructure. All while optimizing for systems that handle life-critical data. It's heavy on innovation and research, with a lot of performance optimization for enterprise-grade healthcare systems.
